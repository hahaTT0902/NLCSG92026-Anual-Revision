# Economics Image Pack

This pack is organized into one folder per unit:

- 3.0 The Allocation of Resources - Supply
- 4.0 Price Determination - Market Equilibrium & Market Disequilibrium
- 5.0 Price Dertermination
- 6.0 Price Elasticity of Demand
- 6.1 PED - Determinants

Also included:

- `OpenStax_Principles_of_Microeconomics_3e.pdf` — full open textbook/courseware source.

## Notes

- Most diagrams are openly licensed files from Wikimedia Commons, many originally from OpenStax.
- The 6.1 folder focuses on visual comparisons that help explain the determinants and outcomes of PED, since that topic is often taught through comparing more elastic vs more inelastic demand shapes.
- I kept the original unit wording you gave, including `Price Dertermination`.
