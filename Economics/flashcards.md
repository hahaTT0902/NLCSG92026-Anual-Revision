# Grade 9 Economics — Flashcards

- **What is scarcity?** The problem of limited resources but unlimited wants.
- **What is opportunity cost?** The next best alternative given up.
- **What are the four factors of production?** Land, labour, capital, entrepreneurship.
- **What is factor income?** Income earned by owning or supplying a factor of production.
- **What is equilibrium?** The point where quantity demanded equals quantity supplied.
- **What is PED?** Price elasticity of demand; responsiveness of quantity demanded to a change in price.
- **What does an indirect tax do?** Increases costs of production and usually shifts supply left.
- **What does a subsidy do?** Lowers costs of production and usually shifts supply right.
