# Immune Response — Study Support

## Core concepts
- Pathogens cause disease.
- White blood cells defend the body.
- Antibodies are specific to antigens.
- Vaccination creates memory cells.

## Revision focus
- non-specific vs specific defence
- phagocytes and lymphocytes
- vaccines and antibiotics
- immune memory

## Suggested revision tasks
1. Draw a simple immune response flowchart.
2. Explain how a vaccine works.
3. Compare antibiotics and vaccines.
4. Make flashcards for antigen, antibody, pathogen and immunity.
