# Respiration — Study Support

## Core concepts
- Respiration releases energy from glucose.
- Aerobic respiration uses oxygen.
- Anaerobic respiration does not use oxygen.
- Gas exchange helps provide oxygen and remove carbon dioxide.

## Revision focus
- aerobic vs anaerobic respiration
- word equations
- lungs and gas exchange
- links between respiration and circulation

## Suggested revision tasks
1. Learn the word equation for aerobic respiration.
2. Compare aerobic and anaerobic respiration.
3. Explain why gas exchange surfaces are efficient.
4. Draw a simple alveolus diagram and label key features.
