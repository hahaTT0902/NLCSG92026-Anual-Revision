# Transport in Humans — Study Support

## Core concepts
- The circulatory system transports oxygen, nutrients and waste.
- The heart pumps blood.
- Arteries, veins and capillaries have different roles.
- Blood has specialised components.

## Revision focus
- double circulation
- blood vessel structure
- roles of plasma, red blood cells, white blood cells, platelets
- coronary heart disease basics

## Suggested revision tasks
1. Label a heart diagram.
2. Compare arteries, veins and capillaries.
3. Explain the role of red blood cells.
4. Write one paragraph on how the circulatory system supports respiration.
