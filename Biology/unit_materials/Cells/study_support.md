# Cells — Study Support

## Core concepts
- Cells are the building blocks of life.
- Plant and animal cells share some structures but also differ.
- Specialised cells are adapted to their roles.
- Tissues and organs are formed from groups of cells.

## Revision focus
- cell structures and functions
- plant vs animal cells
- specialised cells
- cells, tissues, organs and organ systems

## Suggested revision tasks
1. Draw and label a plant cell and an animal cell.
2. Explain one adaptation of a root hair cell.
3. Explain one adaptation of a red blood cell.
4. Make flashcards for nucleus, cytoplasm, membrane, mitochondria and chloroplast.
