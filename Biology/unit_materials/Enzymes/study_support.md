# Enzymes — Study Support

## Core concepts
- Enzymes are proteins that speed up reactions.
- Enzymes have specific active sites.
- Temperature and pH affect enzyme activity.
- Digestive enzymes break down food.

## Revision focus
- active site and substrate
- denaturation
- enzyme specificity
- enzymes in digestion

## Suggested revision tasks
1. Explain the lock-and-key model.
2. Draw a graph of enzyme activity against temperature.
3. Describe what happens when an enzyme denatures.
4. Name three digestive enzymes and what they break down.
