# Grade 9 Biology — Practice Tasks

1. Explain the difference between diffusion and osmosis.
2. Describe how the structure of villi helps absorption.
3. Explain how temperature affects enzyme activity.
4. Compare arteries, veins and capillaries.
5. Describe the roles of red blood cells, white blood cells, platelets and plasma.
6. Explain how vaccines work.
7. Describe how stomata and xylem are involved in plant transport.
8. Design an investigation on how pH affects enzyme activity.
9. Draw a graph from sample data and describe the trend.
10. Evaluate an experiment and suggest three improvements.
