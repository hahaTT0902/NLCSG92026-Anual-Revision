# Biology — Exam Tips

1. Learn definitions exactly: diffusion, osmosis, active transport, enzyme, immunity, vaccination.
2. When explaining adaptations, always link structure to function.
3. For practical questions, identify variables clearly.
4. Use scientific vocabulary precisely.
5. In graph questions, describe trends before explaining them.
6. When comparing systems, use paired points (e.g. arteries vs veins).
7. For digestion, mention enzymes by name where possible.
8. For immunity, separate non-specific and specific defence.
9. For plant transport, do not mix up xylem and phloem.
10. Always give units and label diagrams clearly.
