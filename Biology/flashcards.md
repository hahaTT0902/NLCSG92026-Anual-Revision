# Grade 9 Biology — Flashcards

## Cells and transport
- **What is diffusion?** Movement of particles from a region of high concentration to low concentration.
- **What is osmosis?** Movement of water through a partially permeable membrane from high water potential to low water potential.
- **What is an enzyme?** A biological catalyst made of protein.
- **What happens if an enzyme gets too hot?** It denatures and changes shape, so the substrate no longer fits.

## Digestion
- **What is the role of villi?** Increase surface area for absorption in the small intestine.
- **What does bile do?** Neutralises stomach acid and helps digest fats.

## Circulation and immunity
- **What do red blood cells do?** Carry oxygen using haemoglobin.
- **What do white blood cells do?** Defend the body against pathogens.
- **What is a vaccine?** A preparation that stimulates an immune response and memory against a disease.

## Plant transport
- **What does xylem transport?** Water and mineral ions.
- **What does phloem transport?** Sugars and other dissolved food substances.
