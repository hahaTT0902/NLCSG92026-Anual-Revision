# Grade 9 English — Flashcards

- **What is a simile?** A comparison using 'like' or 'as'.
- **What is a metaphor?** A direct comparison saying one thing is another.
- **What is structure in English analysis?** The way a text is organised.
- **What is tone?** The writer’s attitude towards the subject.
- **What is PEEL?** Point, Evidence, Explanation, Link.
- **What is persuasive writing?** Writing designed to convince the reader.
- **Why proofread?** To correct grammar, spelling and punctuation errors.
