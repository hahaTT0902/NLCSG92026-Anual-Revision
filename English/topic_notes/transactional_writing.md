# English Topic Note — Transactional Writing

## Purpose
Transactional writing is written to explain, argue, advise, persuade, review or inform.

## Audience and tone
Always adapt tone to the audience. A speech, letter, article and review all require different styles.

## Structure
Use a clear introduction, developed paragraphs and a strong conclusion. Topic sentences help organisation.

## Persuasive methods
Use rhetorical questions, direct address, emotive language, repetition, facts and statistics where appropriate.

## Accuracy
Control punctuation, spelling and paragraphing. Keep the purpose clear throughout.

## Exam focus
- Match style to form.
- Keep arguments logical.
- Use persuasive techniques effectively without overloading.
