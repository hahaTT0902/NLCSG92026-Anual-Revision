# English — Key Definitions

- **Simile**: comparison using 'like' or 'as'.
- **Metaphor**: direct comparison saying one thing is another.
- **Personification**: giving human qualities to non-human things.
- **Imagery**: descriptive language that creates mental pictures.
- **Tone**: the writer’s attitude.
- **Structure**: the way a text is organised.
- **Narrative**: writing that tells a story.
- **Persuasive writing**: writing intended to convince the reader.
- **Audience**: the intended reader or listener.
- **Purpose**: the reason a text is written.
- **PEEL**: Point, Evidence, Explanation, Link.
