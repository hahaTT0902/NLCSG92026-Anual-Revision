# English — Exam Tips

1. Read the question twice before writing.
2. Use short quotations and analyse them closely.
3. In analysis, explain effect — do not just name the technique.
4. Keep paragraph focus clear.
5. In creative writing, control tense and viewpoint.
6. Vary sentence structures.
7. Use punctuation deliberately, not randomly.
8. Check spelling of key vocabulary.
9. Leave time to proofread.
10. Match tone and style to audience and purpose.
