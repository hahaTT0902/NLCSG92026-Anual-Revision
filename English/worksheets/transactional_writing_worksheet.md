# English Worksheet — Transactional Writing

## Recall
1. What is audience?
2. What is purpose?
3. Name three persuasive techniques.

## Short tasks
1. Write an introduction to a speech about school stress.
2. Write two persuasive sentences using direct address.
3. Rewrite a weak paragraph to make it more formal.

## Extended
1. Write a short article arguing that students should read more fiction.
2. Plan a letter to the headteacher suggesting one school improvement.
