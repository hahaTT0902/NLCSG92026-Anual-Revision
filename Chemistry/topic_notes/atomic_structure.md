# Chemistry Topic Note — Atomic Structure

## Subatomic particles
Atoms are made of protons, neutrons and electrons. Protons are positively charged, neutrons have no charge, and electrons are negatively charged.

## Relative masses
Protons and neutrons both have relative mass about 1, while electrons have very small mass.

## Atomic number and mass number
Atomic number is the number of protons. Mass number is the total number of protons and neutrons.

## Electron arrangement
Electrons occupy shells. The arrangement of electrons helps determine reactivity and bonding.

## Isotopes
Isotopes are atoms of the same element with different numbers of neutrons.

## Exam focus
- Identify numbers of subatomic particles.
- Use atomic number and mass number correctly.
- Explain what isotopes are.
