# Chemistry — Exam Tips

1. Always write formulas correctly before doing calculations.
2. Learn common ions and atomic masses.
3. Show working clearly in mole calculations.
4. Use balanced equations before applying mole ratios.
5. In bonding questions, mention electrons explicitly.
6. Distinguish clearly between ionic transfer and covalent sharing.
7. For organic chemistry, know the difference between alkanes and alkenes.
8. Use terms like saturated, unsaturated, polymerisation and cracking accurately.
9. Check units in concentration and mass questions.
10. If asked about properties, link them to bonding and structure.
