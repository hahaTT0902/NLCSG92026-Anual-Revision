# Chemistry — Key Definitions

- **Relative atomic mass (Ar)**: weighted mean mass of an atom compared with carbon-12.
- **Relative molecular mass (Mr)**: sum of the relative atomic masses in a molecule.
- **Mole**: amount of substance containing 6.02 × 10^23 particles.
- **Ionic bonding**: electrostatic attraction between oppositely charged ions.
- **Covalent bonding**: sharing of electron pairs between atoms.
- **Metallic bonding**: attraction between positive ions and delocalised electrons.
- **Hydrocarbon**: compound containing hydrogen and carbon only.
- **Alkane**: saturated hydrocarbon with single bonds only.
- **Alkene**: unsaturated hydrocarbon with at least one double bond.
- **Cracking**: thermal decomposition of long-chain hydrocarbons into smaller molecules.
- **Polymerisation**: joining monomers into a polymer.
