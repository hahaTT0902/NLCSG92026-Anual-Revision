# Grade 9 Chemistry — Practice Tasks

1. Calculate relative molecular mass for five common compounds.
2. Convert between mass and moles.
3. Use a balanced equation to calculate product formed.
4. Solve a limiting reactant question.
5. Rearrange a concentration equation.
6. Explain the difference between ionic, covalent and metallic bonding.
7. Draw dot and cross diagrams for ionic and covalent compounds.
8. Describe how fractional distillation separates crude oil.
9. Explain why alkenes are more reactive than alkanes.
10. Describe addition polymerisation and give one example.
