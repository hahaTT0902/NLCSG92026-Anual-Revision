# Grade 9 Chemistry — Flashcards

## Quantitative chemistry
- **What is Ar?** Relative atomic mass.
- **What is Mr?** Relative molecular mass.
- **What is one mole?** 6.02 × 10^23 particles of a substance.
- **How do you calculate moles?** moles = mass / molar mass.

## Bonding
- **What is ionic bonding?** Electrostatic attraction between oppositely charged ions.
- **What is covalent bonding?** Sharing of pairs of electrons between atoms.
- **What is metallic bonding?** Attraction between positive metal ions and delocalised electrons.

## Organic chemistry
- **What is an alkane?** A saturated hydrocarbon with single bonds only.
- **What is an alkene?** An unsaturated hydrocarbon with at least one C=C double bond.
- **What is cracking?** Breaking long hydrocarbon chains into smaller molecules.
- **What is polymerisation?** Joining many small monomers into a long-chain polymer.
