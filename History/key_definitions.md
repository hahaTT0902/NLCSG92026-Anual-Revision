# History — Key Definitions

- **Significance**: importance of an event or factor based on impact or consequence.
- **Appeasement**: policy of giving concessions to avoid conflict.
- **Propaganda**: information used to influence opinion.
- **Segregation**: enforced separation of racial groups.
- **Direct action**: protest methods such as boycotts, sit-ins and marches.
- **Federal action**: action taken by the national government.
- **Treaty**: formal agreement between states.
- **Depression**: severe and prolonged economic downturn.
- **Civil rights**: rights of citizens to political and social freedom and equality.
- **Mandate**: territory administered by another power under international authority.
