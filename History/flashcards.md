# Grade 9 History — Flashcards

- **Why was the Wall Street Crash significant?** It damaged confidence and worsened the Great Depression.
- **What is appeasement?** Giving concessions to avoid conflict.
- **Why did appeasement fail?** It encouraged Hitler and did not stop aggression.
- **What was Brown v. Board of Education?** A 1954 Supreme Court case ruling school segregation unconstitutional.
- **What was the Montgomery Bus Boycott?** A mass protest against bus segregation in 1955–56.
- **What is meant by direct action?** Protest methods such as marches, boycotts and sit-ins.
- **Why has peace in the Middle East been difficult?** Competing claims, security fears, refugees, land and political mistrust.
