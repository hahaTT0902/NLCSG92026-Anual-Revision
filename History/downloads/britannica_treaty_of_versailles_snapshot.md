# Britannica Snapshot — Treaty of Versailles

Source URL: https://www.britannica.com/event/Treaty-of-Versailles-1919

Captured as a text snapshot for revision use.

## Key extracted points
- Signed on June 28, 1919.
- Came into force on January 10, 1920.
- Drafted at the Paris Peace Conference.
- Dominated by the ‘Big Four’: Lloyd George, Clemenceau, Wilson, Orlando.
- Germany had no real say in shaping the treaty.
- Germany lost territory and colonies.
- The treaty included the war guilt clause and reparations.
