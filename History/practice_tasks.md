# Grade 9 History — Practice Tasks

1. Explain why the Wall Street Crash was significant.
2. To what extent was the crash the main cause of the Great Depression?
3. Explain three long-term causes of the Second World War.
4. Why was appeasement adopted?
5. Why did appeasement fail?
6. Which was more significant in the civil rights movement: direct action or legal change?
7. How successful was the civil rights movement by the mid-1960s?
8. Why has peace in the Middle East been difficult to achieve?
9. Was the Treaty of Versailles the main cause of WW2?
10. Write one PEEL paragraph for each of the four units.
