# History Worksheet — Wall Street Crash

## Section A — Recall
1. In which year did the Wall Street Crash happen?
2. What is meant by speculation?
3. Name one weakness in the US economy before the crash.
4. Give one social effect of the Great Depression.

## Section B — Short answers
1. Explain why the Wall Street Crash was significant.
2. Explain how the crash affected ordinary Americans.
3. Was the crash the only cause of the Great Depression? Explain.

## Section C — PEEL practice
Write one PEEL paragraph answering: 
"Why was the Wall Street Crash significant for the USA?"
