# Grade 9 Economics — Practice Tasks

1. Define scarcity and opportunity cost with examples.
2. Explain the four factors of production.
3. Draw and explain a PPF.
4. Draw a demand curve and explain why it slopes down.
5. Draw a supply curve and explain why it slopes up.
6. Show equilibrium on a demand and supply diagram.
7. Explain what happens when there is excess demand.
8. Define consumer surplus and producer surplus.
9. Explain how an indirect tax changes market equilibrium.
10. Explain how a subsidy affects consumers, producers and government.
