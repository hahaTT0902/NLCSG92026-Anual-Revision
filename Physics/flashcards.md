# Grade 9 Physics — Flashcards

- **What is Newton’s First Law?** An object remains at rest or in uniform motion unless acted on by a resultant force.
- **What is Newton’s Second Law?** F = ma.
- **What is Newton’s Third Law?** For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
- **What is momentum?** mass × velocity.
- **What is impulse?** force × time = change in momentum.
- **What is kinetic energy?** Energy of moving objects.
- **What is work done?** Force × distance moved in the direction of the force.
- **What is wave speed?** frequency × wavelength.
- **What is refraction?** The change in direction of a wave when it enters a different medium because its speed changes.
