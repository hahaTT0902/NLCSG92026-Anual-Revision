# Grade 9 Physics — Practice Tasks

1. Define force and explain why it is a vector.
2. State and explain Newton’s three laws.
3. Solve five F = ma questions.
4. Explain momentum conservation in a collision.
5. Define impulse and calculate it in an example.
6. List energy stores and describe energy transfers in a roller coaster.
7. Calculate kinetic energy and gravitational potential energy.
8. Define power and efficiency.
9. Compare transverse and longitudinal waves.
10. Draw reflection and refraction ray diagrams.
