# Physics — Exam Tips

1. Learn equations and units together.
2. In force questions, identify the resultant force first.
3. In Newton’s Third Law answers, state that the forces act on different objects.
4. Show all steps in calculations.
5. Distinguish scalar and vector quantities.
6. For energy questions, name the energy stores clearly.
7. For wave questions, define frequency, wavelength and amplitude accurately.
8. In reflection/refraction questions, use the normal correctly.
9. Draw ray diagrams neatly with arrows.
10. Check final answers for sensible units and significant figures.
