# Physics Worksheet — Forces and Momentum

## Section A — Definitions
1. Define force.
2. Define momentum.
3. State Newton’s Third Law.
4. What is impulse?

## Section B — Calculations
1. A 4 kg object accelerates at 3 m/s². Calculate the force.
2. A 1200 kg car moving at 10 m/s comes to rest. Calculate the change in momentum.
3. A force of 50 N acts for 0.2 s. Calculate the impulse.
4. A 2 kg trolley moving at 5 m/s collides with a stationary 3 kg trolley. Total momentum before collision is conserved. If both move together after impact, find their final speed.

## Section C — Explanation
1. Explain why action and reaction forces do not cancel.
2. Explain why airbags reduce injury.
3. Explain one real-life example of Newton’s Third Law.
